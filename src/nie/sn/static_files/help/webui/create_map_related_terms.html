Directed Results Help:
Related Terms

If you notice that visitors frequently misspell a word, you can provide the correct spelling. Or there may be logically related items you'd like to suggest.

This is done via the optional Related Term(s) box, which lets you suggest related search terms. If set, these words will appear as hyperlinked terms near the top of the results list.

You can also change the lead in text that appears in font of the links. For example, if presenting a corrected spelling, you can change the Intro Text to "Did you mean:".
