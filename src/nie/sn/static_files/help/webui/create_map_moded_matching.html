Directed Results Help:
Optional Sub-Site / Filter Constraints

Many websites have check boxes or drop-down lists on their search forms so that visitors can limit the scope of their searches. For example, a visitor searching for "new products" could limit their search to just the "Press Releases" section of the site.

Normally, SearchTrack suggestions are applied to the entire site, no matter what section the visitor searches in. However, you can override this behavior and have suggestions only apply to sub-sections of your site.

If selected, the Sub-Site / Filter Constraints check boxes will limit when suggestions are shown. You can select multiple check boxes to match more than one section; don't select any check boxes if you want a suggestion to apply to your entire site.
